Frequently Asked Questions

How soon will I receive a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent matters, please call our center directly at +1 (555) 123-4567.

Do I need a consultation before starting treatments?

Yes, all new clients require an initial consultation where our Ayurvedic doctors will assess your dosha type, health history, and wellness goals to create a personalized treatment plan.

What should I bring to my first consultation?

Please bring any relevant medical records, a list of current medications or supplements, and be prepared to discuss your health history, lifestyle, and wellness goals.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations for clients who cannot visit in person. These are conducted via secure video conference and include digital dosha analysis tools.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for appointment cancellations. Late cancellations may incur a fee, except in cases of emergency.
